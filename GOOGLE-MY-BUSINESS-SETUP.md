# Google My Business Setup Guide

## 🎯 Why You Need Google My Business (GMB)

When someone searches "website developer near me" or "web developer in [your city]", your GMB profile can show up in Google Maps and local search results - **completely FREE!**

---

## 📝 Step-by-Step Setup (Takes 15 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Google My Business
1. Visit: https://business.google.com
2. Click "Manage Now"
3. Sign in with your Google/Gmail account

### Step 2: Add Your Business
1. Business Name: **Weekend Dev**
2. Category: Choose "Website Designer" or "Web Developer"
3. Click "Next"

### Step 3: Add Location
**Option A: If you have physical office:**
- Enter your office address
- Customers can visit you

**Option B: If working from home/remotely:**
- Select "I deliver goods and services to my customers"
- Choose your service area (your city or cities you serve)
- Example: Hyderabad, Bangalore, Delhi, etc.

### Step 4: Add Contact Information
- **Phone:** +91 9491376372
- **Website:** Your website URL
- Click "Next"

### Step 5: Verification
Google will verify your business:
- **By Phone:** Get instant code via SMS (fastest)
- **By Email:** Get code via email
- **By Postcard:** They mail verification code (takes 5-7 days)

Enter the verification code to complete setup.
