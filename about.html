About Weekend Dev

Empowering Small Businesses with Affordable Web Solutions

📖 Our Story

Weekend Dev started with a simple observation: small businesses in India were being left behind in the digital revolution.

While big companies could afford ₹50,000-1,00,000 websites from agencies, local cafes, restaurants, and small shops were stuck with either:

  • No website at all (losing customers to competitors)
  • Expensive agencies with 2-3 month wait times
  • Cheap, unprofessional websites that looked terrible

We knew there had to be a better way.

That's why Weekend Dev was born. Our mission is simple: Deliver professional, high-quality websites at prices small businesses can actually afford, without compromising on quality or speed.

💡 Why "Weekend Dev"?

The name reflects our philosophy:

  • Fast Delivery: We deliver in 1 week - about a weekend's worth of focused work
  • Lean & Efficient: No corporate overhead = Better prices for you
  • Dedicated: We treat every project like our own weekend passion project

🎯 Our Mission

"Make professional web development accessible and affordable for every small business in India."

We believe every cafe, restaurant, boutique, and small business deserves a website that:

  • ✓ Looks professional and builds trust
  • ✓ Doesn't cost a fortune
  • ✓ Gets delivered quickly (not 2-3 months!)
  • ✓ Actually helps get more customers
